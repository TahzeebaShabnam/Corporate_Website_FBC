About Us

Our History

The Fijian Broadcasting Corporation Pte Limited is the leading multimedia hub in Fiji and for Pacific Communities. FBC is also Fiji’s national broadcaster delivering compelling content on a range of platforms which include AM radio, FM radio, television and digital media services

FBC was established in 1954 and has evolved from its early days of 18 hours (5am-11pm) of radio broadcast per day to a modern multimedia organization.

Our Products

Our Radio, TV, and Digital Products

Radio Stations

FBC operates six 24-hour radio stations, offering diverse and engaging content in Fiji’s three constitutional languages.

TV Channels

FBC operates three free-to-air TV channels, offering a range of programs including news, sports, entertainment, and educational content.

Digital Products

FBC is expanding its digital footprint, offering on-demand content through its growing range of digital platforms, with additional products currently in development.
