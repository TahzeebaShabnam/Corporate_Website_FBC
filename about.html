About Us

Our History

The Fijian Broadcasting Corporation Pte Limited is the leading multimedia hub in Fiji and for Pacific Communities. FBC is also Fiji’s national broadcaster delivering compelling content on a range of platforms which include AM radio, FM radio, television and digital media services.

FBC was established in 1954 and has evolved from its early days of 18 hours (5am-11pm) of radio broadcast per day to a modern multimedia organization.

Our Core Services

What We Do

Radio Broadcasting

FBC operates six 24 hours radio stations serving Fiji’s three constitutional languages:

  • iTaukei
    • Radio Fiji One (Public Service)
    • Bula FM (Commercial)
  • Hindustani
    • Radio Fiji Two (Public Service)
    • Mirchi FM (Commercial)
  • English
    • Gold FM (Public Service)
    • 2Day FM (Commercial)

Radio Fiji One and Radio Fiji Two are designated Public Service Broadcast (PSB) stations, ensuring nationwide coverage and access to essential information.

Digital Media

The organization is also expanding its digital footprint through VITI+, a streaming platform offering on-demand content since its official launch on February 13, 2025. The Digital team is expanding to maximize on content consumption and will be announcing other digital products soon.

Television Broadcasting

FBC operates three free-to-air TV channels:

  • FBC TV - General entertainment and news in English, iTaukei, and Hindi.
  • FBC 2 - Public service and educational content.
  • FBC Sports - 24/7 sports broadcasting.

News Services

FBC News delivers real-time updates across all FBC’s platforms:

  • TV News - FBC’s flagship daily 6.30 PM TV News bulletin in English and weeknights at 8.30pm, Viti Kei Na Qito, FBC’s prime Itaukei news bulletin.
  • Radio News -12 on the hour radio news bulletins weekdays on the two Public Broadcast Stations and Gold FM. Five radio news bulletins weekdays in three languages is provided to the three commercial radio stations. Three news bulletins feature in the weekends and Public Holidays.
  • Digital News - timely updates on www:fbcnews.com.fj and social media pages

Education

Pacific Multimedia Academy

Pacific Multimedia Academy FBC initiated the Pacific Multimedia Academy which was launched on February 13, 2025 during PMPC 2025, and the World Radio Day. It provides training in TV, Radio and Digital platforms to those in the industry in Fiji and from Pacific Island countries. PMA has strategic partnership with the Fijian National University and BBC Media Action
